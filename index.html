Launch of Prayaas books
Launch of Prayaas books
At Prayaas, we believe that it is never too late to identify the imperfections and work on new ideas. Some of our team members worked for a period of over 2 months to come up with a class-wise set of books that would hence be followed for teaching in the respective classes. In starting of the year 2020, Prayaas launched these books with the motive of increasing the quality of education in all classes. This was certainly a big step capable of taking Prayaas to a next level.

PadBank
initiative
Periods and You| PadBank is an initiative started in the year 2019 with the aim to raise awareness on menstrual hygiene and make sanitary pads available to all. Under this program, workshops are held where the students are educated about the menstrual cycle and how to ensure sanitation during the period. They are distributed free sanitary pads on a monthly basis. So far, the program has witnessed 8 successful editions and we have succeeded in increasing awareness for which the initiative was started.

Swati treatment
Swati Maurya, a student of Prayaas, was suffering from acute anaemia and was admitted in Swaroop Rani Hospital, Allahabad for around 9 days. Besides having other complications, her haemoglobin percentage had dropped down to as low as 5.2%. Her family was not in a position to deal with the situation financially. Prayaas helped the family by providing monetary support and blood units, owing to which the girl was able to recover and got discharged. Swati is one of the best students of Prayaas now and has continued to excel in her examinations.
Admission of Shilpa Diwakar
Admission of Shilpa Diwakar
Shilpa Diwakar, a student of Prayaas, completed her schooling in the year 2015 but unfortunately was not able to go for further studies due to the lack of support and poor financial conditions of her family. But her determination did not break over the years. Team Prayaas succeeded in convincing her family and was able to enroll her for B.Sc, Computer Science at K.P College, Allahabad in July, 2019. Besides, we decided to finance her college education and help her in her studies as much as we can.
