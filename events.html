Diwali in Prayaas every year has been an evening to remember. The pleasing decoration of diyas and rangolis added to the enthusiasm of the kids lighting sparklers and eating sweets is a sight to behold. Even the Prayaas team gets a chance to keep their college pressure aside and get along with the children celebrating the festival of lights.
Holi at Prayaas is more colourful than Gulal. Because in addition to the colours of Gulal are the colours of happiness, innocence and childhood. We celebrate Holi every year with sweets and colours, and with the same heights of excitement as of the young ones. The kids will not let you leave uncoloured, we promise.

Every year on Republic day, we celebrate unity by organizing an afternoon meal for the children, faculty members of the institute and the event volunteers in the mess of Boys Hostel-3. Various types of dishes and sweets are served and the place is decorated with balloons, posters made by the children and rangoli. There is no better feeling than seeing a child go home full and satisfied.
Raksha Bandhan is the day when our bond with the children experiences an exponential increase. Why? Because they are no longer our students, they become our family. Female members of the team tie rakhis to their new little brothers and the male members go to the hostel with their wrists filled from the rakhis by their new little sisters. Every year, we make a new family far from home
Independence day is celebrated in Prayaas with high spirits of patriotism. A program is organized in the Kendriya Vidyalaya campus with the audience as our faculty. Our little ones give various performances on the stage including dancing, singing and poetry recitation, for which they start preparing a week earlier. This is followed by prize distribution by the honâ€™ble director of IIITA to the children who performed well in the year. The celebrations end with the distribution of snacks.
Periods and You|PadBank is an initiative started in the year 2019, wherein girls are educated about maintaining hygiene during menstrual cycles and free sanitary pads are distributed on a monthly basis. There have been 8 successful editions of PadBank and a tremendous increase in the awareness of menstruation and sanitation in the children along with their families.
It marks the day of establishment of Prayaas. It is the day when we look back to the high spirits with which Prayaas was initiated and celebrate the long happy journey it has made evolving over the years. This event is one of the most loved ones by both the kids and the team members. Every year on this day, we organize stalls with fun games in the Kendriya Vidyalaya campus for the kids. Playing games and riding the swings, those happy faces make you want to go back and live your childhood once again.
Health camps are organized timely by Prayaas in collaboration with the health centre of IIITA. Free checkup facility is provided by the doctors of the health centre, where more than 300 children are examined and treated. This is usually followed by lunch arrangement for the kids.
At Prayaas, we believe that even a small act of donation can change someone's life for the better. Following this motto, we have conducted a large number of donation drives over the years collecting clothes, shoes and other useful items from the college faculty and students, and distributing them to the ones in need, be it our kids, people in the nearby village or the railway station.
In addition to knowledge for the growth of mind, a child has to have moral values to be able to grow as a person. Besides, there are some social topics which should be covered in the class by the mentors practically. So in addition to regular teaching, wegular workshops are held in the classrooms on some days by the team members on topics such as cleanliness, child abuse, good touch and bad touch etc.
Remember the times when we were taken out on one-day-trips by the school? Some of the best school memories indeed. A campus visit is something very similar to those one-day-trips. The kids are taken inside the campus of IIIT Allahabad, where they enjoy fun activities all day. A movie in the admin auditorium, snacks, demonstration of robots in the Robotics lab and flying a drone are some of them.
